Frequently Asked Questions

Who can attend DevConf 2026?

Anyone passionate about technology, including developers, students, researchers, entrepreneurs, and IT professionals.

Will sessions be recorded?

Yes. Registered attendees will receive access to session recordings after the conference.

Is there a student discount?

Yes. Students can enjoy discounted tickets with a valid student ID.

What should I bring?

Bring your conference ticket, identification, and a laptop if you're attending workshops or the hackathon.

Can international attendees participate?

Absolutely. DevConf welcomes participants from all around the world.
